Lack of transportation is a major cause of homelessness

Homelessnes is a transportation issue. Without transit, people can't get access to reliable healthcare, get employed, or keep a stable job. Our mission at Pedaling Together is providing homeless in Arizona with reliable transportation in the form of refurbished bicycles. We take donations and host bicycle repairing events.

Transportation is enabling

With access to transportation, the homeless and impoverished can rise. They gain the ability to work. They gain the ability to get housing. They gain stability.

Transportation is a necessity

“For the homeless, reliable transportation can be a way out of their situation—a means of hope. If they can effectively get around the city, they can apply and interview for jobs, visit food banks and access other needed services, and pursue educational goals.”

Backed by studies

Access to reliable transportation is the biggest factor in determining whether someone will escape poverty and avoid homelessness. There is an incredibly strong relationship between transportation and social mobility.
